Dynamite Lumpia

Description:

A deep-fried Filipino appetizer made with chili peppers stuffed with meat filling.

Ingredients:

  • 20 green chilis
  • 500 grams ground pork
  • 1 piece Eden Cheese
  • 2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon white pepper powder
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 20 lumpia wrapper
  • water
  • cooking oil

Steps:

  1. Prepare the green chilis. Slice lenghtwise, remove the seeds and the white part that holds the stem. Wash with cold water.
  2. Prepare the filling. In a bowl, add the ground pork, salt, white pepper powder, onion powder, and garlic powder. Mix well. Slice the cheese into desired thickness.
  3. Assemble the dynamite. Stuff the chilis with cheese. Add the meat filling. Wrap the chilis with lumpia wrapper with the stem outside.
  4. Heat oil under medium low flame. Cook the dynamite for about 3-4 minutes. Do not overcrowd the pan. Serve with your favorite dip sauce.
